Lasanga

Why Lasanga?

Lasanga is a very easy dish to make. Have you made spaghetti? Then you can makes lasanga. There are three ingredients you need; meat, cheese, and pasta . Well, maybe a little more, but just stack those and bake. Pretty easy.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz of Tomato Sauce
  • 16 oz of Cooked Ground Beef
  • 16 oz of Lasanga Noodles
  • 8 oz of Shredded Mozzarella
  • 8 oz of Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 Egg
  • 4 oz of Parmesean Cheese

Steps

  1. Mix the ground beef with tomato sauce in a bot. Let simmer.
  2. In another pot, boil noodles until al dente. (This means "to the tooth", which mean firm to bite)
  3. In a baking dish, layer a small amount of the sauce / meat mixture thinly on the bottom of the dish.
  4. Place a layer of the cooked lasanga noodles on the sauce in the baking dish.
  5. The next layer is a mix of ricotta and mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layering process.
  6. Top the dish with mozzarella and parmesean cheese. Bake in the over for 25 minutes at 375F Degrees.
  7. Let it rest outside of the oven, covered for 15 minutes before serving.
